# Dominion Resources (dominion-resources)

Dominion Resources is the former name of Dominion Energy, one of the nation's largest producers and transporters of energy, providing electricity and natural gas to customers across multiple U.S. states. The company rebranded from Dominion Resources to Dominion Energy in 2017. This repository tracks the legacy brand and redirects to the active Dominion Energy entry.
